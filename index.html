About Us

Ontario Leathercraft, based in Hilton/Parma, NY, started as a passion project and grew into a business dedicated to quality handmade leather goods. We craft wallets and small accessories from premium materials, ensuring timeless durability. Every piece reflects our love for the craft.

Our Products

Materials

All products are made exclusively from vegetable-tanned and chrome-tanned leathers—no synthetics, no imitations, just real leather.

Craftsmanship

Each item is 100% handmade—every piece of leather is measured, cut, skived, and stitched by hand.
