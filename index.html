About

Ontario Leathercraft, based in Hilton/Parma, NY, has been crafting high-quality handmade leather goods for over five years. Specializing in wallets and small accessories, the company was born out of a desire to reconnect with the art of leatherworking. Each product is hand-cut, sewn, and assembled, reflecting a commitment to craftsmanship. Most products are made from vegetable-tanned leather, ensuring that every piece is unique and timeless. Ontario Leathercraft blends functionality with lasting style, celebrating the beauty of handmade artistry.
